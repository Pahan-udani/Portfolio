About Me

I'm a BICT undergraduate at Rajarata University of Sri Lanka, with a strong interest in UI/UX design, frontend development, and quality assurance. My focus is on creating clean, responsive, and user-centered interfaces using tools like Figma, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I enjoy translating ideas into intuitive digital experiences while also ensuring functionality and usability through effective testing. My work reflects attention to detail, consistency in design, and a commitment to delivering high-quality user experiences.

Current interests: responsive UI patterns, heuristic evaluations, and component libraries.

  • Strong foundation in semantic HTML & modern CSS
  • Comfortable with vanilla JS and basic React patterns
  • Eye for detail and accessibility
